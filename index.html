Hello, I am red panda

It is my history

Red pandas, or fire foxes, are fascinating and mysterious creatures that inhabit the mountain forests of South and Southeast Asia. Their history is laden with legends and myths, and they have become a symbol of nature and mystery. Long ago, when the world was still forming, red pandas lived on earth freely and without fear. It is said that they were close friends of wise ancient monks who cared for nature and meditated in secluded temples in the mountains. The monks taught the red pandas respect for the environment and balance. Over time, however, the world has changed. The forests where the red pandas lived began to diminish due to tree felling and road building. The environment began to change and the red pandas had to face new challenges. They began to disappear from their historic habitats.
